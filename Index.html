The Urgent Need: Why P.A.C.T. Matters

Children in Crisis: Unheard Voices

Each year, over 600,000 children in the U.S. experience abuse or neglect. Many of them enter a complex foster care or legal system where their voices go unheard.

600K+ Children Abused/Neglected
~97K Advocates Available

Aging Out: A Harsh Reality & What's Lacking

Every year, about 23,000 youth age out of foster care. The statistics are heartbreaking:

50% Homeless / Unemployed (1st year)
38% Homeless by age 26
30% Law Run-ins by age 21
~70% Men Incarcerated (mid-20s)
